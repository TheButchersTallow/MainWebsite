Shipping Information

Fast, reliable shipping for all your tallow product needs.

Shipping Timeline

Processing Time: Orders ship within 1-2 business days

Delivery Time: 3-7 business days (depending on location)

Orders placed before 2 PM CST Monday-Friday will typically ship the same day!

Shipping Methods

  • Standard Shipping: USPS First Class or Priority Mail
  • Tracking: All orders include tracking information
  • Packaging: Eco-friendly, protective packaging to ensure products arrive safely

Shipping Rates

Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location and order size.

  • Orders under $75: Standard shipping rates apply
  • Orders $75+: Free shipping within the continental US!

Shipping Locations

We currently ship to all 50 United States.

International shipping is not available at this time. We apologize for any inconvenience.

Temperature Considerations

Our tallow products are natural and can soften in high temperatures. During hot summer months:

  • Products may soften during transit but will re-solidify at room temperature
  • This does not affect product quality or effectiveness
  • Store products in a cool, dry place upon arrival

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information.

You can track your package at any time using the provided tracking number.

Questions about your shipment? Contact us and we'll be happy to help!
