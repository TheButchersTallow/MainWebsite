Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

Products

What is tallow?

Tallow is rendered beef fat from grass-fed cattle. It's been used for centuries for skincare, candle making, and household crafts due to its rich nutrient content and versatile properties.

Why use tallow for skincare?

Tallow is remarkably similar to our skin's natural oils, making it highly compatible and easily absorbed. It's rich in vitamins A, D, E, and K, and provides deep, long-lasting moisture without harsh chemicals.

Are your products all-natural?

Yes! All our products are made with 100% natural ingredients. We use grass-fed tallow, organic oils, beeswax, and natural essential oils. No preservatives, parabens, or synthetic fragrances.

Will tallow products make me smell like beef?

No! Our tallow is carefully rendered and purified to remove any beefy smell. Our products have a mild, natural scent, and many are enhanced with pleasant essential oils.

How should I store tallow products?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Tallow is shelf-stable and doesn't require refrigeration. Products may soften in warm temperatures but will re-solidify when cooled.

Ingredients & Sourcing

Where do you source your tallow?

We source our tallow from grass-fed, grass-finished cattle. We work with trusted local Texas suppliers who share our commitment to quality and ethical farming practices.

What does "grass-fed & grass-finished" mean?

Grass-fed means the cattle eat grass their entire lives. Grass-finished ensures they weren't switched to grain before processing. This results in superior quality fat with better nutrient profiles.

Are your products vegan?

No, our products contain tallow, which is derived from beef. However, they are perfect for those following a paleo or ancestral lifestyle.

Usage & Care

How do I use tallow balm?

Apply a small amount to clean, dry skin. The warmth of your skin will help it absorb. A little goes a long way! Use daily for best results.

Can I use tallow if I have sensitive skin?

Yes! Many people with sensitive skin find tallow to be gentle and non-irritating. However, as with any new product, we recommend doing a patch test first.

Will tallow clog my pores?

Tallow is considered non-comedogenic (won't clog pores) for most people. Its similarity to our natural skin oils allows it to absorb well without buildup.

How long do your products last?

Our products have a shelf life of 12-18 months when stored properly. Because they're made with natural ingredients and no preservatives, we recommend using them within this timeframe for best quality.
