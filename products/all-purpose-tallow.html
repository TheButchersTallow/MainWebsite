How to Use

For Candle Making:

  • Melt tallow slowly in a double boiler at low heat
  • Add essential oils for scent if desired (optional)
  • Pour into containers with pre-waxed cotton wicks
  • Allow to cool and solidify completely (2-4 hours)
  • Burn time: approximately 6-8 hours per ounce

For Skincare DIY Projects:

Note: For skincare use, we recommend wet rendering this tallow first for a smoother, more refined texture that's better for skin absorption. Our hand made skincare products use wet-rendered tallow.

  • Melt and combine with carrier oils (jojoba, coconut, almond)
  • Add beeswax for firmer balms (1 part wax to 3 parts tallow)
  • Whip cooled mixture for fluffy body butter texture
  • Mix with essential oils for aromatherapy benefits
  • Create custom lip balms, hand balms, or body moisturizers

For Wood Care:

  • Warm small amount in hands until soft
  • Apply to clean, dry wood surface
  • Rub in circular motions with soft cloth
  • Allow to absorb for 15-30 minutes
  • Buff excess with clean cloth for shine
  • Repeat monthly or as needed for maintenance
  • Perfect for cutting boards, wooden utensils, and wood furniture

Storage Tips:

  • Store in original glass jar at room temperature
  • Keep in cool, dark place away from direct sunlight
  • Shelf life: 12-18 months when stored properly
  • May solidify in cool temps, soften in warm temps
