About This Product

Our 100% All Purpose Suet Beef Tallow is perfect for all-purpose use and skincare. Available in three convenient glass jar sizes to fit your needs. This premium tallow is dry-rendered, perfect for all-purpose use, while our wet-rendered tallow is used in the lip balm and any skincare products.

Why Choose Our Tallow?

  • High smoke point for all-purpose use
  • Rich in vitamins A, D, E, and K
  • Naturally moisturizing for skin
  • Long shelf life when stored properly
  • Sourced from grass-fed cattle

Ingredients

100% Grass-Fed Beef Tallow

Our tallow comes from grass-fed cattle raised on regenerative farms. No additives, preservatives, or chemicals - just pure, natural tallow.

How to Use

For Cooking:

  • Use as a cooking fat for high-heat cooking
  • Perfect for frying, roasting, and sautéing
  • Add to soups and stews for richness

For Skincare:

  • Apply directly to skin as a moisturizer
  • Use on dry, cracked areas
  • Great for hands, feet, and body
