Our Story

At The Butcher's Tallow, we believe in the power of pure, natural ingredients to nourish both body and skin. Our journey began with a life-changing decision: adopting the carnivore diet.

After shedding significant weight and transforming my overall health, I gained a new perspective on what wellness truly means. This led me to scrutinize every ingredient in my life—from the food I ate to the skincare products I used.

I was shocked by the chemicals and fillers in most skincare, so I set out to create something better. Using slow-rendered beef tallow sourced straight from our trusted butcher's shop, I crafted our first product: a deeply hydrating lip balm made with just four ingredients—tallow, beeswax, jojoba oil, and essential oils.

The response was incredible, and we also offer pure, slow-rendered beef tallow for those who value its nourishing benefits. There are 2 types of rendered beef tallow: first being dry-rendered, perfect for cooking with a slight beefy aroma, and wet-rendered (used in the lip balm and any skincare products), adding salt and water to ensure no burning, removing all beefy residue for a clean finish.

Our mission is simple: provide clean, effective skincare rooted in nature's best. We're proud of our small but mighty lineup, and we're excited to grow with plans for tallow-based lotions and sunscreens in the future.

At The Butcher's Tallow, we're not just a brand—we're a commitment to health, transparency, and the power of real ingredients.
